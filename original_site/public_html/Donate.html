Donate

Exhibit Tours Collaborative is a tax-deductible non-profit under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code and donations of cash are qualified for deductions on your tax returns.

Donors may most easily donate by using Paypal which provides you instantly with a printable receipt. You do not need to have a Paypal account in order to make your donation.

Donors wising to donate by check are requested to print the donor form HERE and please enclose with your check so that we may mail you a receipt for your donation.

You can always email us directly: donors@exhibittours.org

At no time will we rent or sell and email addresses to any third party. All contact information is maintained in confidence.
